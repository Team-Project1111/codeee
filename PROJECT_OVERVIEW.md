# 🎨 Kala Heritage - Project Overview

## 🌟 Project Summary

**Kala Heritage** is a comprehensive digital platform designed to preserve, promote, and celebrate traditional Indian folk artforms. The platform bridges the gap between master artists and global audiences while providing modern tools to sustain traditional livelihoods in the digital age.

## 🎯 Problem Statement & Solution

### The Challenge
Traditional Indian folk artforms like Warli, Pithora, and Madhubani are slowly fading due to:
- Limited visibility and reach for local artists
- Lack of modern platforms for promotion
- Difficulty in connecting with wider audiences
- Risk of cultural heritage being forgotten
- Economic challenges for traditional artists

### Our Solution
A fully interactive, user-friendly web platform that:
- **Preserves** cultural heritage through digital documentation
- **Promotes** traditional artforms to global audiences
- **Empowers** artists with modern digital tools
- **Connects** artists with art enthusiasts worldwide
- **Sustains** traditional livelihoods through direct sales

## 🛠 Technical Implementation

### Architecture
- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express.js framework
- **Database**: MongoDB with Mongoose ODM
- **Authentication**: JWT-based secure authentication
- **Security**: Helmet.js, rate limiting, CORS protection

### Key Features Implemented

1. **Professional Profiles**
